"I am an absolute believer that if you can make women’s experiences into data—which doesn't have to be numbers—you can create action." Dr. Valerie Hudson, author and International Affairs professor, on the importance of #DataFeminism. Hudson runs a global database on women, known as the WomanStats Database, which aims to be “the most comprehensive database on the situation and status of women in the world.” Check out the full interview with her on Medium — https://medium.com/data-feminism — and order Data Feminism - the book - from @mitpress today.
